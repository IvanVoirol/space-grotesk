Space Grotesk is a sans-serif typeface and a proportional variant of the original fixed-width Space Mono family. Designed by Florian Karsten in 2018, Space Grotesk retains Colophon's Foundry Space Mono quirkiness and personality but was optimised for better readability at non-display sizes.

Since its initial release in 2018, Space Grotesk was later heavily reworked, and its now a Variable Font that ranges from Light to Bold weights and includes new design features for 2020 Google Fonts release.

Space Grotesk includes Latin Vietnamese, Pinyin, and all Western, Central, and South-Eastern European language support, as well as several OpenType features (old-style and tabular figures, superscript, and subscript numerals, fractions, stylistic alternates).

To contribute, see github.com/floriankarsten/space-grotesk.
